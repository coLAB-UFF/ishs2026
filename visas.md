Visitors from certain countries may need a visa to enter Brazil for short stays, usually up to 90 days for tourism or business purposes. Travelers from the United States, Canada, Australia, India, China, and several countries in Africa and the Middle East are required to obtain a visa in advance.
For detailed visa information, please visit: [https://brazil.vfsevisa.com/](https://brazil.vfsevisa.com/)

If you need an invitation letter to support your visa application, please contact [colab@midia.uff.br](colab@midia.uff.br). Invitation letters are issued only to participants with either an accepted ISHS 2026 submission or a completed conference registration.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out.
