  <h2 class="mb-4">Culture, History, and Architecture</h2>
  <p><strong>Niterói Contemporary Art Museum (MAC)</strong></p>
  <p>An architectural landmark designed by Oscar Niemeyer, MAC hosts contemporary art exhibitions and offers a panoramic view of Guanabara Bay.</p>
  <br>
    <p><strong>Gragoatá Fort</strong></p>
  <p>This historic 18th-century structure provides a strategic view of the bay’s entrance and is a great spot for walks and watching the sunset.</p>
  <br>
      <p><strong>Boa Viagem Island</strong></p>
  <p>A small island featuring a historic church and remnants of colonial fortifications, accessible by a pedestrian bridge and surrounded by scenic views.</p>
  <br>
        <p><strong>Jambeiro Mansion</strong></p>
  <p>A 19th-century residence turned cultural center, featuring art exhibitions, events, and a charming garden.</p>
  <br>
        <p><strong>Niterói Municipal Theater</strong></p>
  <p>A historic venue offering a rich cultural calendar, including plays, concerts, and other artistic performances.</p>
  <br>
  <br>
    <h2 class="mb-4">Nature and Outdoor Recreation</h2>
  <p><strong>Gragoatá Waterfront</strong></p>
  <p>A scenic seaside promenade ideal for walking, jogging, and enjoying views of Rio de Janeiro across the bay.</p>
  <br>
    <p><strong>Arrows Beach (Praia das Flechas)</strong></p>
  <p>A small urban beach with calm waters—perfect for relaxing and appreciating the coastal landscape.</p>
  <br>
    <p><strong>St. Benedict’s Field (Campo de São Bento)</strong></p>
  <p>A vibrant city park with green spaces, a lake, artisan markets, and areas for leisure and outdoor activities.</p>
  <br>
    <p><strong>Niterói City Park (Parque da Cidade)</strong></p>
  <p>Located atop Morro da Viração, it offers spectacular viewpoints, picnic areas, and a launch site for hang gliding.</p>
  <br>
      <p><strong>Niemeyer Path (Caminho Niemeyer)</strong></p>
  <p>A waterfront architectural complex designed by Oscar Niemeyer, featuring several cultural venues and public spaces.</p>
  <br>
---

There are several leisure options in the vicinity of UFF. Visitors can enjoy a variety of cafes, restaurants, and small shops within walking distance of the campus. For those who appreciate outdoor activities, nearby parks and waterfront areas offer pleasant spots for walking, jogging, or simply relaxing. The surrounding neighborhood also features cultural attractions, including local art galleries and small theaters, providing a taste of Niterói’s vibrant cultural scene.
