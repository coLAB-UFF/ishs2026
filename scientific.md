The Scientific Committee of ISHS 2026 plays a consultative and honorary role, providing guidance and expertise to support the academic quality and intellectual rigor of the conference. Committee members contribute their insights on program development, review of submissions, and the overall scholarly direction of the event.

Their participation is voluntary and serves to strengthen the conference’s academic standards, ensuring that ISHS 2026 reflects the highest levels of scholarly excellence in humor studies. We are grateful for their dedication and invaluable contributions.
