  <h2 class="mb-4"><a href="https://hungaralanches.com/">Húngara Lanches</a></h2>
  <p>Despite its name, this spot is proudly local, famous for hearty sandwiches and homemade sauces. Try the “Hungarian” burger — a spicy Brazilian twist that reflects the area’s playful culinary inventiveness. Another very popular option is the Italiano, a type of baked savory or empanada, with cheese and ham.</p>
  <br>
  <h2 class="mb-4"><a href="https://hungaralanches.com/">Sara Lanches e Pratos</a></h2>
  <p>A local favorite for its generous portions and friendly service, Sara Lanches is a food truck which serves classic Brazilian snacks such as x-tudo burgers, pastel, and açaí bowls. It’s the kind of place where you taste the everyday flavor of Niterói’s student life. Its most popular option is the famous 37cm pastel.</p>
  <br>
  <h2 class="mb-4"><a href="https://hungaralanches.com/">ETs - Bar e Lanchonete Niterói</a></h2>
  <p>A quirky, bohemian hangout blending sci-fi décor with Brazilian boteco vibes. ETs Bar is best known for its bold snacks — from loaded fries to calabresa sandwiches — and its laid-back atmosphere after sunset.</p>
  <br>
  <h2 class="mb-4"><a href="https://hungaralanches.com/">Canta Lanches</a></h2>
  <p>Known for its pastel com caldo de cana (fried pastry with sugarcane juice), a classic of Brazilian street-food culture, Canta Lanches captures the essence of a quick snack between classes and panels.</p>

UFF’s Gragoatá Campus features several small cafeterias spread across its academic buildings. Popular choices include Cantina da Maura and Maia Restaurante. There are also affordable off-campus options nearby. The quickest alternatives can be found at Cantareira Square, located right in front of the university’s main entrance.

Cantareira Square is a well-known bohemian spot in Niterói, popular among students and locals alike. There you’ll find food trucks and casual bars that are perfect for socializing with friends. For a more upscale experience, the Reserva complex offers restaurants serving gourmet snacks, pizzas, and artisanal sandwiches.
