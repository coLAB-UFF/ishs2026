The university is located near a cultural center that features movie theaters and fancy restaurants, just a two-minute walk from campus. Additionally, close to the main entrance of the Gragoatá is Largo da Cantareira, a lively bohemian square with a variety of bars and eateries offering outdoor seating, popular among young people. About 15–20 minutes away on foot, visitors will also find Plaza Shopping, one of the city’s main malls, which offers several dining options and other amenities.
