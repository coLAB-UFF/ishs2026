We are pleased to introduce the members of the International Society for Humor Studies (ISHS) Board, whose expertise and leadership guide the society and its activities worldwide. Their contributions are consultative and honorary, ensuring the highest academic standards in humor research and conference programming.

These members represent a wide range of disciplines, reflecting the interdisciplinary nature of humor studies. Their experience and guidance are essential for shaping the scholarly direction of ISHS conferences and advancing research in humor across the globe.
