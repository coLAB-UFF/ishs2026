If you are a graduate student conducting research in humor studies, consider applying to the ISHS Graduate Student Awards (GSA). The award of US$ 250 aims to recognize outstanding contributions to the field. The recipients will also have the chance to present a paper at a special ceremony during the next ISHS Conference.

In order to be eligible to the award, you must:

•	be an ISHS student member working toward a masters or doctoral degree 

•	register for and attend the 2026 ISHS Conference

•	submit an independently written conference paper (papers co-authored by an advisor are ineligible)

Please email all documents in a single file to <a href="mailto:humorstudies@midia.uff.br">humorstudies@midia.uff.br</a> with “Student Awards” as the subject line:

•	an application letter indicating that you are competing for the Graduate Student Award and a description of your research program within humor studies

•	a curriculum vitae

•	a conference paper abstract by <strong>May 15, 2026</strong>
